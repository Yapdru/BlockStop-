Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? ▼

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a password reset link. Click the link and create a new password.

How do I cancel my subscription? ▼

You can cancel anytime from Settings → Billing. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise customers.

How long does file scanning take? ▼

Most files are scanned within 1-5 seconds. Large files (over 100MB) may take up to 30 seconds. Results are typically instant.

Is my email data stored? ▼

No. We analyze email content in real-time but do NOT store the actual email text. Only metadata and threat analysis results are saved to your account for history purposes.

Can I use BlockStop for work? ▼

Absolutely! Our PRO and MAX tiers include team features. You can add up to 5 team members on PRO, and unlimited on MAX.

What happens if I find a security vulnerability? ▼

Please report security issues responsibly to security@blockstop.io. Include detailed information about the vulnerability and steps to reproduce it. Do not publicly disclose the issue.

How do I export my data? ▼

Go to Settings → Privacy → Data Export. Select the data types you want to export (scans, history, etc.) and we'll prepare a JSON file for download.
