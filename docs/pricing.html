Frequently Asked Questions

▼ Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

▼ Is there a free trial?

Yes, our FREE tier is available indefinitely, though with scan limits. For paid plans, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

▼ Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for teams and enterprise customers. Contact our sales team for custom quotes.

▼ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise customers.

▼ How does the scan limit work?

Scan limits reset daily at midnight UTC. Each email or file scanned counts as one scan. Unlimited plans have no restrictions.

▼ Can I share my account with family members?

We recommend using our team features for this. PRO and MAX plans support multiple team members with their own accounts.

▼ What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is safely retained for 90 days. You can request export or deletion anytime. Read our privacy policy for details.

▼ Do you offer custom enterprise solutions?

Yes! Our MAX tier and custom enterprise plans support on-premises deployment, API whitelabeling, and more. Contact sales@blockstop.com
