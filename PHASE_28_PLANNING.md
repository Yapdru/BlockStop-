**Status:** Planning Phase

## 📋 Executive Summary

Phase 27 successfully delivered advanced analytics, threat intelligence, enterprise features, RBAC, SSO/SAML, and audit logging. BlockStop is now positioned for strategic evolution.

Phase 28 represents a critical inflection point where we must decide:
- **Direction:** Where should BlockStop focus next?
- **Scale:** Who is our primary user segment?
- **Geography:** Which markets should we prioritize?
- **Technology:** What competitive advantages should we build?

This document outlines potential directions and asks essential clarification questions to define Phase 28's scope, timeline, and success criteria.
